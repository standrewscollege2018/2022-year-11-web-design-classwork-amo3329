Rush

The Band Members

Rush was a Canadian progressive rock band formed in Toronto in 1968. It went through several configurations of band members
until it reached the classic trio in 1974. These three band members were Geddy Lee, Alex Lifeson, and Neil Peart.
In 2018 they annouced that they were done.

Geddy Lee

Geddy Lee was born 1953.
He was the lead vocalist, bassist and keyboardist.

Alex Lifeson

Alex Lifeson was born in 1953.
He was the guitarist and backing vocals

Neil Peart

Neil Peart was born in 1952.
He was the drummer and primary lyricist.
He died of gliobastoma in 2020.
